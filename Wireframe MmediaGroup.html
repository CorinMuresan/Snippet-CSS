Typeface & convention

The typeface is highly suggested to be used in all:

  • Marketing material
  • Representations of the company

All titles on internal and external documentation must follow the following convention:

  • Be distinguishable either by size or weight
  • IBe clear and consice
  • Be short
  • Have only the first letter capital, unless due to gramatic synax

Paragraphs on all internal and external communication, unless done for explicit reason, must:

  • Not exceed 75 characters per line
  • Be a minimum of 40 characters per line
  • Be clearly distinguishable from the title
  • Use the Roboto light typeface
